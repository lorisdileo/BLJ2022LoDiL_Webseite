Nutellabread

ingredients

10 tablespoon nutella

100 mililiters milk

100 grams sugar

2 eggs

20 mililiters oil

250 grams flour

1 teaspoon baking powder

1 pack of vanilla sugar

preparation


First you take the Nutella and make small piles. You put them on a plate, then in the fridge for a few hours.


Mix milk, sugar, oil, and the two eggs in a bowl. The ingredients must be mixed well.

Mix 1 teaspoon with vanilla sugar with flour. Stir the flour with the milk mixture. Knead the dough until the dough becomes firm.

After kneading, let the firm dough rest for 2 hours.

After resting, roll out the dough. Preheat the oven to 150, top and bottom heat with convection.

Cut out round cookies with a mold.

Place the Nutella pieces on the round cookie. Coat the Nutella with the dough and place it on a baking sheet.

Place the tray in the oven for 20 minutes.
